AI Programming

I believe that great AI is the cornerstone of a living, breathing game world. My work in AI focuses on creating believable, challenging, and optimized agents that enhance the player's experience, rather than simply acting as obstacles. I have extensive experience with Behavior Trees, State Machines, and custom pathfinding solutions.

System: Dynamic Squad Tactics (Cybernetic Echo)

For this project, I designed and implemented the AI for squads of enemy soldiers. The goal was to make them feel like a coordinated unit that could react to the player's high-mobility playstyle.

Key Features:

  • Behavior Trees: Utilized Unreal Engine's Behavior Trees to create complex decision-making logic. AI agents can dynamically switch between offensive, defensive, and support roles.
  • Cover System: Agents can identify and utilize cover points in the environment, peeking out to fire and suppressing the player's position.
  • Flanking & Pincer Maneuvers: Squad members communicate their positions and intentions, allowing them to organize and execute flanking maneuvers on the player.

System: Creature Ecosystem (Aetherion's Gate)

Designed a simple AI ecosystem for the procedurally generated dungeons, where different monster types have unique behaviors and interactions with each other and the environment.

Key Features:

  • Finite State Machines (FSM): Used a lightweight FSM for creature AI, defining states such as 'Idle', 'Patrol', 'Chase', and 'Special Ability', which was highly performant for a roguelike with many agents.
  • Faction System: Implemented a simple faction system, allowing for infighting between different monster types, which the player can use to their advantage.
  • Environmental Awareness: AI agents are able to recognize and react to environmental hazards, such as avoiding traps they have seen the player trigger.
